Who is Hozier?

Andrew John Hozier-Byrne, known professionally as Hozier is an Irish musician. He was born in Bray, County Wicklow, Ireland. Currently 34 years old, Hozier has been releasing music since 2013 when he realeased his smash hit single "Take me to church"
